Connect your murshid

The murshid reads your sahifa and responds to where you are. To activate it, enter your Anthropic API key. Your key is stored only in this browser and never sent anywhere except directly to Anthropic.

Get a key at console.anthropic.com — the murshid uses claude-sonnet-4-6. Without a key, you can still use all sahifa features; the murshid panel will show example responses.
